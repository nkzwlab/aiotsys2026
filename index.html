AIoTSys 2026

24 - 26 August Hakodate City, Hokkaido, Japan
The 4th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence of Things and Systems

Welcome to AIoTSys 2026

With the rapid advancement and convergence of Internet of Things (IoT), big data, and artificial intelligence (AI) technologies, the integration of AI into IoT (AIoT) has emerged as a promising frontier with substantial developmental potential. This dynamic field is increasingly reshaping and deepening its impact across a wide range of economic and social domains, driving the transformation of traditional industries into a new era of intelligence that goes beyond mere digitization and connectivity.
The 4th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence of Things and Systems, AIoTSys 2026 is dedicated to exploring the intersection of AI and IoT. This conference will bring together leading researchers, industry experts, and practitioners from around the world to share cutting-edge research, insights, and innovations. AIoTSys 2026 will feature a highly selective program of technical papers, along with panel discussions, and keynote speeches. We invite high-quality submissions presenting original research that advances the state of the art in ubiquitous intelligence and computing. Peer reviewed and presented papers will be published into AIoTSys 2026 International Conference Proceedings, which will be indexed by EI Compendex.
